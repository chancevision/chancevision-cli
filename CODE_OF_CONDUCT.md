# Code of Conduct

We want ChanceVision CLI to be a welcoming project for contributors with different backgrounds, skill levels, and perspectives.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps the community:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying clear about impact.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Welcome questions from new contributors.
- Respect privacy and do not share private data, API keys, or images.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publicly sharing private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.
- Dismissing contributors instead of addressing the substance of their work.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants who violate these standards. If you see a problem, contact the maintainers privately.
